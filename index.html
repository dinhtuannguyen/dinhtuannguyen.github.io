About me

    Dinh Tuan Nguyen received the BS degree and MS degree from Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology (State University), Russia Federation, in Electronic Computers, Computer Engineering, in 2010 and 2012, respectively. From 2013 – 2016, he was a Teaching Assistant at the Faculty of Radio-Electronics, Le Quy Don Technical University, Hanoi, Vietnam. Since 2016, he has been with the Faculty of Radio-Electronics, Le Quy Don Technical University, Hanoi, Vietnam, where he is currently a Senior Lecturer. His research interests are Digital Logic Circuit Analysis and Design, Microprocessing Engineering, Computer Network and Computer Architecture.
